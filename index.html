About Me

I am a medical student at Michigan State University College of Human Medicine. My research focuses on orthopedic outcomes, preoperative risk prediction using machine learning, and disparities in healthcare. I also co-founded Mobilize Africa, a nonprofit that delivers medical and educational supplies to schools and clinics in underserved regions in West Africa through partnerships with Michigan State University and local institutions.

Current Projects

Machine Learning & Deep Learning in Surgical Care

Applying advanced computational methods to clinical datasets to identify early predictors of postoperative complications, with the goal of improving risk stratification and decision-making in surgical care.
